Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Repoverse ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • User ID (anonymous identifier)
  • Onboarding preferences (interests, tech stack, skill level, goals)
  • App usage data and interactions

1.2 Repository Data

  • Saved repositories
  • Repository interactions (swipes, likes, passes)
  • Viewed repositories history

1.3 Subscription Information

Subscription status and purchase history are handled by Apple App Store and Google Play Store. We receive subscription status through RevenueCat but do not store payment information.

1.4 Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash reports and error logs (for app improvement)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalization: To provide personalized repository recommendations based on your preferences
  • Service Delivery: To deliver and improve our services
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the App and improve user experience
  • Subscription Management: To manage your subscription status and provide premium features

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat processes subscription data in accordance with their privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

3.2 Supabase

We use Supabase for backend services, data storage, and authentication. Your data is stored securely in Supabase's infrastructure.

3.3 Apple App Store / Google Play Store

Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple and Google. We do not have access to your payment information.

3.4 GitHub API

We fetch public repository data from GitHub API to provide repository information. We do not access your private GitHub data.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely in Supabase databases
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We implement security measures to protect against unauthorized access

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can request access to your personal data
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and data
  • Correction: You can update your preferences and information through the App
  • Opt-out: You can opt-out of certain data collection (where applicable)
  • Export: You can request a copy of your data

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our mobile app does not use cookies. We may use analytics tools to understand app usage patterns, but this data is anonymized and aggregated.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your personal information).

12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to data portability and the right to object to processing of your personal data.
